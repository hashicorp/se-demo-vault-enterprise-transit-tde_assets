Approach Benefits
Security Model: Adopt a zero-trust security posture and remove single-points-of-failure in the protection chain for DEK and KEK. Attackers cannot exploit exfiltrated data when database servers do not expose their unencrypted Data Encryption Key and Key Encryption Key.
Centralized Workflow: Establish and enforce strong guidelines to rotate encryption keys, re-encrypt data at rest, and validate data integrity with automation. Database servers adhere to a centralized, automated workflow to manage the lifecycle of Data Encryption Keys and Key Encryption Keys.
Strong Governance: Enforce a centralized audit trail to provide visibility to ensure key management lifecycle is complainant and reported. Teams establish authentication and authorization processes for the lifecycle of encryption keys, enforced by policies that support audit and control standards.
